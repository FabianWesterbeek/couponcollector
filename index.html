Coupon Collector Calculator

Enter a drop rate (e.g. "0.1" or "1/15") and the number of coupons (e.g. "24"), then click Calculate.

Explanation

Given a drop rate \( \frac{1}{r} \) and \( n \) coupons, the expected number of tries to collect all coupons is given by:

\[ E[Y_n] = r \cdot n \sum_{i=1}^{n} \frac{1}{i}. \]
